Autonomous Robot

Clean &Living in a world of robots

This robot is designed to autonomously navigate by following a designated line on the ground. The line typically contrasts with the background (e.g., black line on white surface or vice versa)..

Working Principle: 1.The line sensors constantly scan the ground for the designated line. 2.The microcontroller receives data from the sensors, determining the robot's position relative to the line (centered, deviating left, or deviating right). 3.Based on the sensor data, the microcontroller sends signals to the motors, adjusting their speed or direction. 4.If the robot strays off the line, the microcontroller corrects its course by controlling the motors to bring it back onto the line. Industrial Automation: In factories, line-following robots can transport materials or perform inspections along designated paths.
